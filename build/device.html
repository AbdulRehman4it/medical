Never Miss A
Dose Again

In a world full of distractions, it's easy to overlook what truly matters—our health. For some, a missed medication dose may cause discomfort, but for others, it can lead to life-threatening complications.

Over half of all adults in the United States take daily prescription medication. Whether it's managing arthritis, diabetes, or heart disease, strict adherence is crucial. Missing even one dose can transform a small mistake into a major health crisis.

How Pillgram Works

1

Timed Alerts and Reminders

Pillgram sends alerts through the device and the app at the scheduled time. A photo
of the pill appears on-screen, and the corresponding pill holder lights up, guiding you
to the correct dose.

2

Pill Verification

Confirm intake using the device or the app, ensuring that each dose is logged and
tracked accurately.

3

Caregiver Notifications

If a dose is missed, a designated caregiver is immediately notified via the app,
allowing for quick intervention.

4

Real-Time Monitoring

Monitor medication adherence from anywhere, ensuring safety even when you're not
physically present.
