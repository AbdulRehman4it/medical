Pillgram

The smart medication management system that will give you and your family peace of mind.

Never worry about missed doses, or medication errors again with Pillgram’s automated AI- assisted pill recognition and real-time monitoring systems.

Never Miss A Dose Again

Pillgram is not just another pill dispenser; it is a comprehensive, AI-assisted
medication management solution that integrates with a user-friendly app to provide
patients and caregivers peace of mind. The system ensures doses are never missed,
tracking and verifying every intake with visual guidance and timed alerts.

Pillgram’s key Features Include

PILL VERIFICATION ON SCREEN

On-screen image of medication, name, what it is prescribed for and prescriber.

REAL-TIME ALERTS

Reminders are sent to patients and the caregivers about the next doses via the app and the Pillgram devices.

VERIFY MEDICATION IS TAKEN

The system allows the care team to track if and when medication has been taken. Lowering medication mistakes.

THE APP

Pillgram App shows all medication information and scheduling to the care team.
